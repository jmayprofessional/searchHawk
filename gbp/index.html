Google Business Profile Guide

Follow these steps in order to complete your Core 30 setup and optimize your business for maximum local SEO.

1. Business Name

Ensure your business name is consistent everywhere.

2. Business Description

Craft a clear description signaling purpose & location.

3. Primary Category

Set the core category that matches your main service.

4. Secondary Categories

Add supporting services without confusing Google.

5. Address & Service Area

Set location & service areas accurately.

6. Attributes & Hours

Add relevant attributes and accurate hours.

7. Services

List all services clearly to help Google understand your offerings.
