Monitoring and Updates

Active monitoring helps catch errors early, maintain rankings, and improve user trust.

Checklist

  • Monitor GBP for suspensions or warnings
  • Track website uptime and broken links
  • Update service offerings as they change
  • Refresh content on homepage, service pages, and location pages annually
  • Check NAP consistency across citations regularly
  • Track review responses and post updates weekly
