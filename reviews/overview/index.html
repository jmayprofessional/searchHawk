Reviews & Engagement Overview

Reviews and engagement are the core proof that your business exists and provides real services. Properly managing this section improves rankings and clicks.

Google Values

  • Review volume & velocity
  • Responses to reviews
  • Posts & updates
  • Q&A activity

Checklist

  • Understand why reviews impact local rankings
  • Commit to regular engagement (weekly or bi-weekly)
  • Prepare system for tracking reviews and responses
