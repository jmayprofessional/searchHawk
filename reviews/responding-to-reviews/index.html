Responding to Reviews

Show Google and customers that you actively manage your profile and value feedback.

Checklist

  • Respond to every review, positive or negative
  • Keep responses natural and polite
  • Include service mentions lightly (avoid keyword stuffing)
  • Avoid copy/paste responses
  • Respond in a timely manner (24-48 hours ideal)
