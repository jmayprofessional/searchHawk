Address and Service Area

Correct location and service areas help Google understand where you operate and display your business locally.

Checklist

  • Physical location set accurately (or hidden if Service Area Business)
  • Service areas set to actual cities served
  • Avoid including states, counties, or unnecessary zip codes
  • Confirm address consistency with website & citations
