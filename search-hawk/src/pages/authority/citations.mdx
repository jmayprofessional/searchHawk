# Citations

**Purpose:** Local citations signal to Google that your business is real and trustworthy. A citation is any mention of your business name, address, and phone online.

**Checklist:**

- [ ] Submit business info to major local directories (Yelp, BBB, Angi, etc.)
- [ ] Ensure NAP is consistent across all citations
- [ ] Include correct primary category where possible
- [ ] Add business to relevant industry-specific directories
- [ ] Track all citations in a spreadsheet for audits
- [ ] Avoid duplicate or low-quality citations that can confuse Google
