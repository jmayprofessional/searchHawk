# Social Profiles

**Purpose:** Social profiles reinforce your brand, NAP, and website links for local SEO trust signals.

**Checklist:**

- [ ] Create/claim profiles on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn (at minimum)
- [ ] Ensure NAP matches GBP exactly
- [ ] Add link to website on each profile
- [ ] Use consistent branding (logo, images, business description)
- [ ] Post updates periodically to signal activity
- [ ] Include other niche social profiles relevant to your industry

**Notes:**  
*(Optional: maintain a posting calendar and engagement tracker)*
