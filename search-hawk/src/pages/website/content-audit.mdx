      Content Audit for Local SEO
    </h1>
    <p class="text-gray-300 text-lg mb-4">
      Reviewing your website content ensures it fully aligns with your Google Business Profile and helps you rank higher in local search results. Each step below explains why it's important and how to implement it effectively.
    </p>

  {/* Audit Sections */}
  <div class="w-full max-w-5xl grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Keyword Mapping</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Assign a primary keyword (service + city) to each page. This tells Google exactly what each page is about.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> Homepage → "Plumber Charleston" | Service Page → "Emergency Plumbing Charleston". Use the keyword in the H1, meta title, and URL.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">NAP Consistency</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Your business name, address, and phone number (NAP) must match exactly across your site and GBP. Inconsistencies confuse Google and hurt rankings.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> If your GBP lists "123 Main St, Charleston, SC", don’t write "123 Main Street" elsewhere.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Meta Titles & Descriptions</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        These tell search engines and users what each page is about. Including service + city improves click-through and local relevance.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> Title → "Emergency Plumbing in Charleston, SC | Fast 24/7 Service" <br/>
        Meta Description → "Need an emergency plumber in Charleston? We’re available 24/7 for leaks, clogs, and more. Call today!"
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Content Hierarchy & Readability</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Use H1 for your main service + city, H2 for sub-services or benefits, and bullets for readability. Proper hierarchy helps Google understand your page structure.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> <br/>
        H1: "Plumber in Charleston, SC" <br/>
        H2: "Emergency Plumbing Services" <br/>
        Bullet Points: "Leak Repairs", "Drain Cleaning", "Pipe Installation"
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Internal Linking & CTAs</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Link related service pages and your homepage. Clear calls-to-action guide users to contact you, boosting conversions and engagement metrics (which indirectly impact SEO).
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> Add links like "Learn more about our drain cleaning services" and CTA buttons: "Call Now" or "Request a Quote".
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1 md:col-span-2">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Trust Signals</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Reviews, badges, and links to authoritative sources build trust with Google and your visitors. This improves rankings and click-throughs.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> Embed Google reviews, display Better Business Bureau or industry certifications, link to local business directories.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1 md:col-span-2">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Monitoring & Updates</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        SEO isn’t a one-time setup. Regularly check rankings, update content for new services, and ensure your GBP info matches.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Tip:</strong> Schedule audits monthly for active services and quarterly for evergreen content.
      </p>
    </div>
