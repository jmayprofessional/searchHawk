    <h1 class="text-5xl font-extrabold bg-clip-text text-transparent bg-gradient-to-r from-purple-400 via-pink-500 to-red-500 mb-4">
      Homepage Alignment
    </h1>
    <p class="text-gray-300 text-lg mb-4">
      Your homepage is often the first thing both users and Google see. Align it with your Google Business Profile (GBP) so it clearly communicates your primary service, location, and credibility.
    </p>
    <p class="text-gray-400">
      Each section below explains why it matters, how to implement it, and examples to follow.
    </p>
  </div>

  {/* Checklist Sections */}
  <div class="w-full max-w-4xl grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">H1 Matches GBP Primary Category</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Your main heading (H1) tells Google and users what your page is about. Matching it to your GBP primary category reinforces local relevance.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> H1 → "Plumber in Charleston, SC"
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Include City or Service Area</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Mention your location naturally in headings, intro text, and services. This signals to Google where you operate.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> "We provide expert plumbing services in Charleston, SC and surrounding areas."
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">List Main Services Clearly</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Display your main GBP services on the homepage so users can quickly find them and Google can match searches.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> "Emergency Plumbing", "Drain Cleaning", "Water Heater Repair"
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Use Natural Language (No Keyword Stuffing)</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Write for humans first. Overusing keywords can hurt readability and rankings.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> Instead of "Plumber Charleston Plumbing Services Charleston Plumber", write "Our Charleston plumbing team is ready to fix leaks, clogs, and more."
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1 md:col-span-2">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Confirm Homepage NAP Matches GBP</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Consistent Name, Address, Phone (NAP) ensures Google trusts your business location.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> GBP lists "123 Main St, Charleston, SC" → homepage must use the exact same format.
      </p>
    </div>

  </div>
