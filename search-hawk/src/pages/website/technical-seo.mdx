    </p>

  {/* Checklist Sections */}
  <div class="w-full max-w-4xl grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Mobile-Friendly & Fast Loading</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Google prioritizes mobile-friendly, fast websites. Slow or broken mobile pages can hurt rankings.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>How to do it:</strong> Use responsive design, compress images, enable caching, and minimize heavy scripts.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> Test using Google PageSpeed Insights and aim for 80+ mobile score.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">SSL Certificate (HTTPS)</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        HTTPS secures your site, protects visitors, and is a ranking factor.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>How to do it:</strong> Install an SSL certificate (most hosting platforms offer free options like Let's Encrypt).
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> Your site should load as <code>https://yourdomain.com</code>, not <code>http://</code>.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Structured Data (Schema)</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Adding LocalBusiness schema helps Google understand your business details and display rich results.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>How to do it:</strong> Include name, address, phone, hours, service area, and services in JSON-LD format.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> Google Rich Results Test tool can validate your schema.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Meta Titles & Descriptions</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Titles and descriptions tell Google what your page is about and encourage clicks.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>How to do it:</strong> Include main service + city in title and description naturally.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> Title: "Plumber in Charleston, SC | Fast & Reliable". Description: "Expert plumbing services in Charleston. Call today!"
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">URL Structure</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Clean URLs improve usability and local SEO signals.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>How to do it:</strong> Use lowercase, dashes for spaces, include service + city.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> <code>/plumber-charleston</code> or <code>/drain-cleaning-charleston</code>
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Internal Linking</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        Links help Google understand page relationships and hierarchy.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>How to do it:</strong> Link homepage → service pages → related services.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>Example:</strong> On homepage: "Our Services → Emergency Plumbing, Drain Cleaning"
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="p-6 bg-gray-800 rounded-xl shadow-lg hover:shadow-2xl transition transform hover:-translate-y-1 md:col-span-2">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mb-2">Sitemap & Robots.txt</h2>
      <p class="text-gray-400 mb-2">
        These files guide search engines to index the right pages.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-400">
        <strong>How to do it:</strong> Create <code>sitemap.xml</code> with all pages, and <code>robots.txt</code> allowing search engines to crawl them. Submit sitemap in Google Search Console.
      </p>
    </div>
