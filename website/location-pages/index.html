Location Pages

If you serve multiple cities or areas, create a page for each location to mirror your GBP service areas.
Proper setup helps your business rank locally in multiple areas.

Checklist for Each Location Page

  • Create one page per city or service area served
  • Include consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) for that location
  • Match service mentions to your GBP offerings
  • Ensure unique content on each location page to avoid duplicate content
  • Include internal links to relevant service pages and homepage

Optional: List all target cities and map them to individual pages to keep your site organized and improve local SEO coverage.
