NAP & Schema

Structured data (LocalBusiness schema) and consistent NAP information signal legitimacy to Google.

  • Name, Address, Phone match GBP exactly
  • LocalBusiness schema implemented on homepage
  • Add schema to location & service pages where relevant
  • Include “sameAs” links for social profiles
  • Verify schema with Google Rich Results Test

(Optional: keep a log of schema URLs and testing results)
