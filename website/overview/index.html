Website Alignment Overview

Your website should mirror your Google Business Profile (GBP) so Google sees consistent signals about your business.
Proper alignment improves your chances of ranking in local search results and the map pack.

Key areas to focus on include NAP consistency, service and location matching, structured content, and schema markup.

Checklist for Website Alignment

  • Confirm your website domain matches your GBP.
    Why: Google trusts consistent domain info across profiles.

  • Check NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across the site and GBP.
    Why: Inconsistent info confuses Google and can hurt rankings.

  • Identify main services to mirror on the website.
    Why: Google matches your site content to searches for your services.

  • Plan location pages if serving multiple areas.
    Why: Each service + location combination can rank separately in local search.

  • Review website for schema markup opportunities (LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ).
    Why: Schema helps Google understand your business and display rich results.

Optional: Write down your main services and service areas for website alignment planning.

Example:

  • Primary Service: Plumbing → Homepage H1: “Plumber in Charleston, SC”
  • Service Pages: “Emergency Plumbing Charleston”, “Drain Cleaning Charleston”
  • Location Pages: “Plumber in Summerville, SC”, “Plumber in Mount Pleasant, SC”
